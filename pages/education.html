Education

Children of Pakistan face the dual problem of educational inequality with limited access to schools and poor quality schooling. The rest on account of poverty drop-out in between. This has led to an education crisis in Pakistan and has led Cutchi Memon Markazi Jamaat Pakistan to provide scholarships to bring quality in the field of education. Cutchi Memon Markazi Jamaat Pakistan Educational Scholarship is given to deserving students community members studying at the recognized schools or colleges. Scholarships are being granted for admission fee, tuition fee, cost of two uniforms and cost of books, once in a year, renewable and based on performance criteria with securing 60% marks and above (in case of new admission of Prep & Nursery classes, the condition of 60% performance criteria will not be applicable) The student aid program can be extended and further renewed, entirely making it subject to performance and securing admirable grades. Eligibility criteria is applicable on house hold income of less than 50,000 Under this facility, the deserving student is eligible for admission fee (provided that it is mandatory) and tuition fee of one year. The grant is renewable provided that the students excels academically and secures 60% marks or higher.

Higher Education

Students of Pakistan are facing problems of educational inequality with limited access to universities and low-quality education facilities. The rest is on account of poverty drop-out in between. This has led to an education crisis in Pakistan and has led Cutchi Memon Markazi Jamaat Pakistan to provide scholarships to bring quality in the field of education Cutchi Memon Markazi Jamaat Higher Education Scholarship is given to deserving students of Cutchi Memon community members studying at the recognized schools or colleges. Scholarships are being granted for admission fee, tuition fee and renewable on the basis of performance with criteria of securing either 60% marks or 2.7 CGPA and above in previous examinations. The student aid program can be extended and further renewed, entirely making it subject to performance and securing admirable grades. Eligibility criteria is applicable on those who are hold of income less than monthly salary of Rs. 75,000/- Under this facility, the deserving student is eligible for an admission fee (provided that it is mandatory) and a tuition fee of one year. The grant is renewable provided that the student excels academically and secures a CGPA of 3.2 and above.
